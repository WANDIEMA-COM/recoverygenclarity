Meet Your Coach

Nimrod Kiprotich

BSc. Exercise Physiology & Rehabilitation Specialist

"Exercise is medicine, but only when prescribed correctly." That is the core philosophy that drives my practice.

As a Clinical Exercise Physiologist, I don't just give you a workout plan. I analyze your biomechanics, understand your medical history, and design highly specific interventions to treat chronic diseases, recover from catastrophic injuries, and optimize human performance.

Whether you are a stroke survivor learning to walk again, an athlete returning from an ACL tear, or someone managing diabetes through movement, my goal is to give you the tools and physiological adaptations to thrive.

Areas of Expertise

Targeted Physiological Interventions

We utilize advanced physiological testing and targeted exercise programming to address complex physical challenges.

Cardiac & Stroke Rehab

Safe, monitored exercise progression for patients recovering from heart attacks, surgeries, or neurological events to restore cardiovascular efficiency and motor control.

Chronic Disease Management

Exercise prescriptions specifically designed to manage and improve outcomes for Diabetes (Type II), Hypertension, Osteoporosis, and Metabolic Syndrome.

The Genesis Protocol

We don't guess. We assess. Our four-step clinical pathway ensures that your exercise prescription is safe, effective, and continuously adapting to your recovery.

1

Clinical Assessment

Comprehensive review of your medical history, biomechanical screening, and baseline physiological testing (VO2, strength, mobility).

2

Exercise Prescription

Developing a highly specific, dosage-based exercise plan outlining exact modalities, intensities, frequencies, and durations.

3

Guided Intervention

One-on-one coaching to ensure perfect form, appropriate neuromuscular activation, and safety during all movements.

4

Re-evaluation & Progression

Continuous monitoring of metrics to progressively overload your system, driving continuous physiological adaptation and recovery.

"The adaptation is always specific to the imposed demand. We ensure the demand is exactly what your body needs to heal."
